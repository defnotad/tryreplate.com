How It Works

Three steps. No new process.

Replate is designed to sit alongside your existing operations — not inside them.

1

You set aside tonight's surplus

Before 6pm, your team sets aside the day's unsold items as surprise boxes. No fixed quantity. No consistency required. You decide what goes in. We manage everything through WhatsApp — no app needed on your end.

2

Customers reserve on Replate

Users in your area see your box on the app and reserve it before closing. You receive confirmed demand — and confirmed revenue — before the day ends.

3

They collect. You earn.

Customers arrive with an alphanumeric code. Your team hands over the box in under 30 seconds. Revenue reaches your account within 24 hours.
